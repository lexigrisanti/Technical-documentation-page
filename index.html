Welcome

Rich in history and natural beauty, Buena Vista offers the kind of small-town life most people only dream of. If you're a current resident, you've already experienced the closeness of the community.

“Buena Vista” which means “beautiful view” in Spanish, is pronounced BYOO-nah VIS-ta by most locals. It was named by Alsina Dearheimer, who suggested the name with its unique pronunciation when it was incorporated as a town in 1879. basically:
